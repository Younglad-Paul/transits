Our Story

Transits Global Charity is a non-profit organization founded in July 2022, with headquarters in the United States of America and a branch office in Nigeria. Born out of compassion, we were established with the mission of improving the lives of the poor and vulnerable in Africa and beyond, including the elderly and those struggling financially.

Currently focused on serving Africa with Nigeria as our pilot location, we are also working to establish home programs in Houston, Texas, USA. As our name suggests, we envision expanding our reach to serve communities globally in the future.

Our mission extends to providing comprehensive support through:

  • Emergency relief and assistance for vulnerable individuals
  • Shelter and support for widows and elderly individuals
  • Food security and nutrition programs
  • Community development and empowerment initiatives
